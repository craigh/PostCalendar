## PostCalendar v8.0.0 Menu Configuration

Link to PostCalendar by entering <tt> {PostCalendar:user:display} </tt> in a menu block.

Go to your Block administration and edit/create the Menu block to hold your link to PostCalendar.

The Menu Block editing screen should look something like this:

Name         | URL                         | Title
-------------|-----------------------------|------------
PostCalendar | {PostCalendar:user:display} | My Calendar
